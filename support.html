💬 Support & FAQ

Ayra — Video Player for Kids

📱 Getting Started

How do I add local videos?

Open Ayra, go to your playlist, and tap the Add Video button. You can select videos from your device's photo library or files. Videos stay on your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How do I import a YouTube playlist?

To import a YouTube playlist:

  1. Find the YouTube playlist you want to import
  2. Copy the playlist URL or ID
  3. In Ayra, tap Add Playlist → YouTube Import
  4. Paste the URL and tap Import

Ayra fetches the playlist via RSS feed — no YouTube account or login required.

What are emoji playlists?

Emoji playlists let you organize videos with fun emoji icons instead of boring text names. Kids can recognize their favorite playlists by the emoji — like 🦕 for dinosaur videos or 🎵 for music!

🔐 Parental Controls

How do unlock methods work?

When Guided Mode is active, kids can't exit the player without solving an unlock challenge. You can choose from 4 methods:

  • PIN Code — Enter a 4-digit PIN you set
  • Easy Math — Simple addition/subtraction (e.g., 3 + 4)
  • Hard Math — Harder problems (e.g., 17 × 3) — stumps most kids!
  • Pattern Lock — Draw a pattern you set

You can change the unlock method anytime in Settings.

What is Guided Mode?

Guided Mode locks the player so your child can only watch the videos you've selected. They can't browse, exit to other apps, or skip past your set limits. Perfect for screen time you feel good about.

What is DND (Do Not Disturb) Mode?

DND Mode prevents notifications and interruptions while your child is watching. No pop-ups, no calls breaking through — just uninterrupted viewing.

What are skip limits?

Skip limits let you control how many times your child can skip to the next video. This encourages them to actually watch what's playing instead of endlessly flipping through videos.

🔧 Troubleshooting

A video won't play

Try these steps:

  • Local video: Make sure the file format is supported (MP4, MOV, M4V work best). Try playing the video in your phone's default player to confirm it's not corrupted.
  • YouTube video: Check your internet connection. The video may also have been removed or made private by the creator.
  • Force-close Ayra and reopen it
  • Restart your device if the issue persists
I forgot my PIN / pattern

Delete and reinstall the app to reset all settings, including your unlock method. Since Ayra stores everything locally and collects no data, a reinstall gives you a fresh start. You will need to re-add your playlists.

YouTube playlist import isn't working

Make sure:

  • The playlist is set to Public (not Private or Unlisted)
  • You're pasting the full playlist URL
  • You have an internet connection

Some very large playlists may take a moment to load.

The app is crashing
  • Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem continues, email us with your device model and iOS version
