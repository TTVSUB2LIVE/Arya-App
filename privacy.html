🔒 Privacy Policy

Ayra — Video Player for Kids

Effective: February 1, 2026

Ayra is designed with your family's privacy as the top priority. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none) and how the app works.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Ayra has:

  • No user accounts or login
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No server or backend — the app runs entirely on your device
  • No cookies, identifiers, or fingerprinting

Local Video Access

When you add local videos, they are accessed directly on your device. Ayra does not upload, copy, or transmit your files anywhere. All playlist data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

YouTube Content

Ayra allows you to import YouTube playlists via RSS feeds. When YouTube videos are played, content is loaded from YouTube's servers. This means:

  • YouTube/Google's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply to that content
  • Ayra itself does not send any data to YouTube — we only read publicly available RSS feeds
  • We have no control over data YouTube may collect when videos are played

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Ayra is designed for use by parents and children together. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by not collecting any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no data transmission of any kind from our app.

Third-Party Services

Ayra does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporters, or ad networks. The only external service interaction is loading YouTube content when you choose to play YouTube videos.

Data Storage

All app data (playlists, settings, unlock preferences) is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is removed. We never have access to this data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated effective date. Since we don't collect contact information, we encourage you to check this page periodically.
